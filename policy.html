Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data Train With Buse ("we", "us", "our") collects when you use our website (trainwithbuse.com), book services, purchase programs, or communicate with us — and how we handle that data.

By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. What Data We Collect

Data you provide directly

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number — when you book a session, subscribe to our newsletter, or send us a message.
  • Health & fitness information: age, gender, fitness level, goals, physical limitations, pregnancy status — when you take our quiz or begin a training program. This helps us create a safe and effective plan for you.
  • Payment information: processed securely by third-party providers (Calendly, Payhip). We never store your credit card details directly.

Data collected automatically

  • Usage data: pages visited, time on site, referral source — collected via Cloudflare analytics.
  • Device data: browser type, operating system, screen size, IP address.
We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Ever.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalize our training services
  • To process bookings and payments
  • To communicate with you about your sessions and programs
  • To send you our newsletter (only if you opt in — you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • To improve our website and services
  • To ensure your safety during training (health data)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following services that may process your data:

  • Calendly — for booking sessions
  • Payhip — for program purchases and payments
  • Cloudflare — for website hosting and analytics
  • Formspree — for newsletter signup forms
  • Substack — for blog and newsletter delivery

Each of these services has their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

4. Data Retention

We keep your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill legal obligations. If you request deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain it.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR (as we are based in Berlin, Germany), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@trainwithbuse.com.

6. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for functionality (Cloudflare, Calendly embed). We do not use advertising cookies or trackers. No data is sold to ad networks.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Session Rules

Last updated: February 2026

To get the most out of our time together, here are some guidelines for all training sessions.

Before Your Session

  • Wear comfortable workout clothing and appropriate shoes.
  • Have water available throughout the session.
  • Eat a light meal at least 1–2 hours before training.
  • Inform me of any injuries, pain, or health changes before we start.
  • For online sessions: ensure a stable internet connection, enough space to move, and your camera on (so I can check your form).

During Your Session

  • Sessions are 60 minutes unless otherwise agreed.
  • Communicate openly — tell me if something hurts, feels wrong, or if you need a break.
  • I'll push you, but your safety always comes first. We train smart, not reckless.
  • Be present — minimize phone use during the session.

After Your Session

  • Follow any recovery recommendations I give you.
  • Track your progress if applicable (I'll guide you on how).
  • Reach out if you experience any unusual pain or discomfort post-session.

In-Person Sessions (Berlin)

  • Sessions can take place at your home, a gym, or an outdoor location — we decide together.
  • If training at your home, please ensure enough clear space (approximately 2x2 meters).
  • I bring any equipment we need for home sessions.
  • Parking costs (if applicable) are not included in the session fee.

Online Sessions

  • We use Zoom or Google Meet — I'll send you the link before the session.
  • If you experience technical difficulties, let me know immediately so we can reschedule if needed.
  • Sessions are not recorded unless you request it and give consent.

Pregnancy & Postpartum

If you are pregnant, recently postpartum, or planning to become pregnant, please let me know before we begin. I will adapt all exercises for your safety. A doctor's clearance is recommended before starting or continuing a training program during pregnancy or postpartum recovery.

Medical Conditions

If you have any pre-existing medical conditions (heart conditions, diabetes, joint issues, etc.), please disclose them before your first session. This information is kept strictly confidential and is only used to ensure your safety during training.

Any questions before we start?

Don't hesitate to ask — I want you to feel comfortable and prepared.
