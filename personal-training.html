Questions

Common questions.

Do I need to be in Berlin for in-person training? +
Yes — in-person sessions are held in Berlin. If you're outside Berlin, 1:1 Online Coaching is the perfect alternative with the same level of personalization and access to Buse.
What equipment do I need for online coaching? +
Nothing mandatory. Programs are designed around what you have access to — whether that's a full gym, a set of dumbbells, or just your bodyweight at home.
Is in-person training really referral-only? +
Yes. I keep my in-person client roster intentionally small to give each client full attention. That said, you can still reach out — spots occasionally open up and I'll add you to the waitlist.
How is this different from a regular personal trainer? +
I bring a sports science MSc, an NSCA-CPSS certification, and ongoing research work at Charité Berlin to every session. Programming is grounded in neuroscience and movement science — not just reps and sets.
Can I combine online coaching with The Lab System? +
Absolutely — Lab System access is included with online coaching. Many clients use the Lab programs to train independently between their coaching sessions.
